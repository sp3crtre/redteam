WHOAMI

Cybersecurity student with a strong focus on red teaming, network penetration testing, and Active Directory attack chains. Currently pursuing OSCP, CRTO, and PNPT certifications while grinding HTB labs, building a home lab, and competing in CTFs.


My methodology follows a structured kill chain: reconnaissance to map the attack surface, enumeration to find misconfigurations and weak points, exploitation for initial access, lateral movement and privilege escalation to reach the crown jewels, and thorough documentation to turn every finding into a clear, actionable writeup.


Home lab runs a full Active Directory environment with multi-domain controllers, Splunk SIEM, and dedicated C2 infrastructure for adversary simulation. Every box and lab exercise gets documented to contribute back actionable knowledge to the security community.


Deepening my skills in evasion techniques, AV/EDR bypass, and custom exploit development. Long-term goal is to lead offensive security operations and help organizations harden their defenses through real-world adversarial emulation.
